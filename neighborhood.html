Central Pointe Apartments

Central Pointe Apartments in Charlotte, NC are now offering NEWLY RENOVATED 1, 2 and 3-bedroom apartment homes! Located on Central Ave at the edge of Plaza-Midwood, Charlotte’s trendiest area, we are close to some of the city’s best food spots like Skrimp Shack, Soul Central, Halal Street Food, Redeye Diner and Papi Queso. With Uptown, NoDa, Cotswold and Matthews nearby, you’ll never have to go far. Central Pointe Apartments offers easy access to I-77, US HWY 74 and beyond through CATS public transit.
